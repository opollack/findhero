Media Inclusivity

Although superhero movies and comics have become more inclusive in the past five years with the creation of the Black Panther and WonderWoman movies, Superheros are prominently white males.

This quickly becomes an issue with children who idolise and look up to superheroes as role models. It becomes a struggle to find a superhero who looks like them, celebrates the same holidays as them, or has the same disability as them.

Growing up with a role model that's very different from you culturally can cause an identity crisis and sometimes a rejection of their own culture.
